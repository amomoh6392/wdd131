Study in Germany - My Site Plan

Site Name

Study in Germany

Proposed Domain Name: mystudyingermany.com

I chose this name because it clearly identifies the purpose of my website. The site is designed to provide useful information for international students who are interested in studying in Germany, including universities, scholarships, student life, and the application process.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to help prospective international students learn about educational opportunities in Germany. The site will provide information about admissions, scholarships, visa requirements, accommodation, and student life.

Scenarios

  • What are the requirements for international students to study in Germany?
  • Which scholarships are available for international students in Germany?

Color Schema

Navy Blue (#1E3A5F) - Used for headings, navigation, and important sections of the website.

Gold (#FFCC00) - Used for buttons, links, highlights, and accents.

Light Blue (#EEF4FA) - Used as the page background to provide a clean and professional appearance.

Typography

Playfair Display - Used for headings and titles.

Poppins - Used for body text and general content.
